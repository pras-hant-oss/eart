Our Story

A Taste of the Himalayas in Kathmandu

Baagan Thakali Kitchen brings the authentic flavors of the Thakali people from the Mustang region of Nepal to the heart of Kathmandu. Our recipes have been passed down through generations, preserving the unique culinary traditions of the Himalayas.

We use only the freshest local ingredients and traditional cooking methods to create dishes that transport you to the mountain villages of Upper Mustang. From our signature Thakali Dal Bhat to our hand-picked local spices, every meal is a journey.
